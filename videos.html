Chef's Pasta

MS Paint, the new Photoshop

This video was a sped up video of my using Microsoft Paint as photoshop, how is that you may ask? Well I didn't own photoshop at the time, and I discovered the ability of the shift key, which allows for your current selection to be used as a brush! This video is very special to me, as it is (as of April 2021) one of my most viewed videos on my channel! I have no idea how it was recommended to so many people, it's not even that good of a video, but it makes me super happy nonetheless!
