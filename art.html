This was a project where I was tasked with making this really high resolution photo of me have a transparent background! This helped me learn about using the polygonal lasso tool, as now I know exactly how it is used for maximum efficiency!

Balance Photo

Here we were taking with taking a photo that represents balance in a photo! Here I got my friends of similar proportions to stand together, as it would be very neatly balanced!

Black and White to Color

Using the Balance photo, we were taked with making an image black and white, and then restore the color using Photoshop! I had no idea how to do this before, but now it feels that I can bring life to just about any old photo!

CD Cover Project

In this project we were asked to create out very own CD Cover using what we learned in Photoshop! Of course I decided to have fun with this! In Wii Music there is a CD Cover you create after each song, so I got with my friends and created some silly CD covers with them, and then we choose our favorite, and we recreated it!

Editing Assignment

Here we were taught the basics of editing photos to make them pop out more, such as lighting, and color correction! It's not bad for a first time!

Final Project

This was my final project for my photography class! We were told to create a complex document that represented our favorite quote from a song! I chose the quote "You can do anything you want to" as heard in 009 Soundsystem "Dreamscape". This quote meant a lot to me, as not only was it a song from my childhood back from the old days of watching youtube videos, but it's very inspirational to me, as it makes me feel that if I put my mind towards something, I can achieve whatever I want, and to never give up!

Framing Photos

Here we were learning about framing photos, where objects in the foreground make their own frame for the subject matter! Here my friend can be seen as if he was amerging from the trees!

Landscape Photos

Here we had to go around campus and take photos in landscape mode, as in the photo is primarily horizontal, which is great for landscape shots! Here I took a photo of the eagle statue at school, as I always admired it's beauty, plus I never really got the chance to take a really good look at it!

Light and Shadow Photo

For the light and shadow picture I took, I took a photo that perfectly showed a contrast between light and shadow in a picture, to which I took a photo with flash in the mirror giving a big thumbs up! This creates the flash of the camera being a bright light illuminating me, but not the background as it'f further away, creating the feeling that I can shine even when things may appear dark!

Portrait Photos

With simplicity photos, photos are to be taken that doesn't have a whole lot going on, and I wanted to be really creative with mine! What I did was set up a scene as if there is an invisible man, to which I used fishing line and tape to make a "floating" hat and glasses!
